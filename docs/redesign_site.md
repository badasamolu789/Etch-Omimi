# Typography

The typography is one of the biggest branding tools.

## Headings

A modern editorial serif.

Examples:

* Canela
* Cormorant Garamond
* Editorial New
* Instrument Serif

Large.

Elegant.

Spacious.

---

## Body

A clean sans-serif.

Examples

* Inter
* Manrope
* Geist
* General Sans

The contrast between serif headings and sans body creates a premium magazine aesthetic.

---
